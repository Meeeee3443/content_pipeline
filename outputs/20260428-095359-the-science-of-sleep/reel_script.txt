Did you know that our brains are hard at work while we're sleeping? During REM sleep, your brain processes memories and consolidates learning. Non-REM sleep helps repair and rejuvenate your body. But what about those weird dreams we have? Research suggests that dreams are a result of our brain's attempt to make sense of the world. A great bedroom can be the difference between a restful night's sleep and a restless one. Consider investing in blackout curtains, a comfortable mattress, and a cool temperature to promote better sleep.
