High-temperature industrial furnaces are the backbone of many manufacturing processes, but they can also be a major source of waste heat. By harnessing the power of this wasted heat, industries can reduce their energy bills and improve their bottom line.

emiSEAL is a game-changing solution that leverages advanced ceramic materials to create a high emissivity coating for industrial furnaces. This innovative technology reflects and radiates heat back into the furnace chamber, reducing energy absorption and improving radiant heat transfer efficiency.
